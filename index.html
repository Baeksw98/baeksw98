Sang Won Baek

I work as an AI Research Scientist at Sionic AI. I am currently leading a multimodal LLM research project to optimize AI reasoning and content detection in various file formats. Prior to joining Sionic, I worked as a Medical Data Scientist at Samsung Medical Center and Seoul National University Hospital, primarily conducting research on developing and validating healthcare AI tools. My research interests include Real-World Data Analytics, Predictive Modeling, Explainable AI, Interpretable ML, Multimodal Learning, Data Mining, and Data Visualization. My primary objective is to develop AI-based precision healthcare systems that streamline clinical workflows and enhance patient care. My primary research objectives are as follows:

  1. Deliver interpretable and reliable AI tools to clinicians to provide a holistic view of disease progression, thereby facilitating clinical workflows and enhancing patient care.
  2. Develop a precision healthcare system using advanced AI tools to generate detailed patient profiles from multimodal physiological data, optimizing the use of comprehensive information for personalized treatment.
